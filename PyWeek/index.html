About

PyWeek2020 is an initiative taken up by the students of TKM College of Engineering, Kollam along with IEI association.
It celebrates a week with coding and experiential learning and gives an opportunity to interact with professionals in order to learn the basics of Python and other related subjects. The key objective of this program is to make students feel comfortable and to get rid of all the myths and fears associated with coding. It will make people realise the strength and simplicity of Python language and change them from being amateurs to confident Pythonistas.
PyWeek2020 is divided into different events and competitions. Events include, learning Python, Django basics and advanced, OOP concepts, open CV and an informal session where the participants can interact with the coordinators. Some mind captivating competitions will also be conducted to test the skills. Students can participate and share their views right from the comfort of their home.
